Sedifex is a POS and operations platform for retail and service businesses.

Who this guide is for

  • Owners/Admins managing setup, billing, and growth channels.
  • Staff/Cashiers handling sales, customers, and bookings.

Core workflow

  1. Sign in and confirm the correct workspace/store.
  2. Use role-based navigation (owner vs staff).
  3. Run operations in Sell, Customers, Bookings, and Business records.
  4. End day with close checks and follow-up messages.
