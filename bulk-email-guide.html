What Bulk Email is for

Use Bulk Email for newsletters, announcements, offers, and long-form customer communications.

Setup checklist

  • Confirm sender identity in Account → Integrations.
  • Prepare segmented audience lists.
  • Write a clear subject line and single call-to-action.

Send workflow

  1. Create campaign draft.
  2. Preview on desktop/mobile.
  3. Send test email to internal inboxes.
  4. Schedule/send to the final segment.

Best practices

  • Use concise copy and scannable headings.
  • Avoid sending duplicate campaigns.
  • Track opens/clicks and improve each campaign.
