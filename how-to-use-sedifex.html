Sedifex Navigation & Daily Operations Guide

Sedifex is a POS and operations platform for retail and service businesses. This page reflects the current app navigation, role-based access, and the main workflows teams use every day.

View navigation Daily checklist Troubleshooting
Who this guide is for

Owners / Admins

Set up and manage billing, integrations, catalog structure, team access, and growth channels.

Staff / Cashiers

Handle sales, customers, bookings, and business records during day-to-day operations.

Step 1

Sign in and confirm workspace access

  1. Open your Sedifex app URL, for example https://app.sedifex.com.
  2. Sign in with your approved account.
  3. Confirm your active workspace or store.
  4. If you manage multiple stores, switch from the workspace selector before starting work.
If data looks missing after sign-in, first confirm that you are in the correct workspace and role.

Main sections

What each page is used for

Dashboard (owner)

Use Dashboard for an at-a-glance view of sales, trends, and current priorities.

  • Review today’s performance snapshot.
  • Check progress against recent periods.
  • Jump quickly to items, SMS, and other actions.

Start each day here to set priorities.

Items

Use Items to manage your product and service catalog.

  • Add or edit item name, category, price, and stock.
  • Maintain barcode and SKU values for quick selling.
  • Keep descriptions and images clean for team clarity and customer receipts.
Tip: Use a consistent naming style for size, unit, and variant format.

Sell

Use Sell for checkout and transaction processing.

  1. Search or scan item.
  2. Add to cart and adjust quantity.
  3. Confirm totals and discounts.
  4. Select payment method.
  5. Complete sale and issue receipt.
  • Close day for reconciliation and shift-close checks.
  • Invoice for formal billing and invoice sharing.

Customers

Use Customers to build and maintain customer records.

  • Add and update customer profiles.
  • Link customers to sales and follow-ups.
  • Improve reminder, delivery, and campaign targeting quality.

Accurate contact data reduces failed communication.

Bookings

Use Bookings for appointment and schedule management.

  • Create bookings.
  • Update booking statuses.
  • Keep notes and timing accurate for team handoffs.

Consistent status updates improve service reliability.

Blog

Use Blog to publish content and promotions tied to your store.

  • Share offers and updates.
  • Publish featured products.
  • Keep public-facing content fresh.

SMS

Use SMS for targeted customer outreach.

  • Segment recipients before sending.
  • Keep messages short and clear.
  • Check usage or credits before launch.
  • Avoid duplicate campaigns.

Bulk email

Use Bulk email for longer-form campaigns and announcements.

  • Prepare audience and subject.
  • Draft campaign content.
  • Verify sender and delivery setup in Account → Integrations.
  • Send and monitor responses.

Use email for richer content and SMS for short or urgent reminders.

Business records

Use Business records to manage business income and expense records.

  • Log expenses with meaningful labels.
  • Keep records current for reporting and reviews.
  • Support profit tracking alongside sales.

Update records daily to avoid end-of-month backlog.

Public page

Use Public page to manage your customer-facing store profile.

  • Update store details and contact channels.
  • Control public catalog visibility.
  • Publish promotions and brand content.

Account (owner)

Use Account for workspace controls and setup.

  • Billing and subscription management.
  • Integrations and API keys.
  • Team and workspace configuration.
  • Security and delivery channel setup.

Review account settings weekly.

Step 3

Daily operating checklist

Start of day

  • Confirm internet and device readiness.
  • Review Dashboard or pending tasks.
  • Check critical stock in Items.
  • Confirm cashier and staff access.

During operations

  • Process transactions in Sell.
  • Keep customer records current.
  • Update Bookings in real time.
  • Log expenses in Business records.

End of day

  • Run Close day checks.
  • Review pending invoices.
  • Complete SMS and email follow-ups.
  • Confirm public updates are live.
Step 4

Quick troubleshooting

I can’t find Product tab

It is now Items.

I can’t find invoice

Open Sell and use the Invoice option.

My navigation looks different

Your account role controls which pages are visible.

Campaign sending is blocked

Check SMS credits and confirm setup in Account → Integrations for email or SMS services.

My public details are wrong

Update them in Public page, then confirm the changes were saved and published.
